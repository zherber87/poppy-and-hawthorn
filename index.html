Our Story

A family-inspired
business, born of love.

Poppy & Hawthorn came to life shortly after our founder welcomed her daughter into the world — a way to stay close to family while building something meaningful with her hands.

The name is drawn from our birth flowers — a poppy and a hawthorn — a small, personal detail that shapes everything we make. Each board is assembled with care, creativity, and the belief that food can hold memory.

— Alexandria & team Founders
